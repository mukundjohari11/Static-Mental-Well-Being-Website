What is Mental awareness?

Mental health awareness is a crucial initiative aimed at educating individuals about mental health issues , reducing stigma , and encouraging open conversations.it encourages people to prioritize their mental health.Our goal is to create a readily available space for people who are suffering from mental issues irrespective of societal norms.Mental health is a state of mental well-being that enables people to cope with the stresses of life, realize their abilities, learn well and work well, and contribute to their community. It is an integral component of health and well-being that underpins our individual and collective abilities to make decisions, build relationships and shape the world we live in. Mental health is a basic human right. And it is crucial to personal, community and socio-economic development.In conclusion, mental awareness is a foundational aspect of mental health that impacts individual and collective well-being. As we continue to prioritize mental health, cultivating mental awareness becomes imperative for fostering healthier individuals and communities.

Symptoms of mental illness?

The persistent felling of sadness or loss of interest .Manic episodes may include symptoms such as high energy reduce need of sleep and loss of touch of reality,it may also include low motivation and loss of interst in daily activities people may also experience behavioural changes such as hyper activity , self harm irritability and some cognitive changes such as dilusion unwanted thoughts and false belief. Physical symptoms can accompany mental health disorders as well. Individuals may report unexplained aches and pains, fatigue, or changes in appetite and weight.In conclusion, recognizing the symptoms of mental illness is vital for promoting mental health and facilitating timely intervention.Awareness and understanding of these symptoms can empower individuals to seek help, fostering a culture of openness and support for those facing mental health challenges.
